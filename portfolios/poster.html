POSTER DESIGN COMPETITION

Counselling Cell IITR organised a Poster Making Competition. The design of the winning entry became the face of Counselling Cell IITR and will be hosted in the campus from the next session in all the Bhawans. We received numerous posters for the Poster Design Competition. Out of these our team chose 5 posters for prizes which were Sennheiser Headphone & Sandisk USB 32GB Flash Drive. The winning entry can soon be found all around the campus.
